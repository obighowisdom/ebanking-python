Features & benefits

  • Borrow from £1,000 to £50,000

    • Subject to our assessment of your personal financial circumstances.

  • Quick decision

    • No matter how you apply you'll get an instant credit decision (subject to planned maintenance periods).

  • Fixed repayment terms

    • You always know how much you need to repay each month.

  • Repayment holidays

    • You could have the option of a repayment holiday for the first three months or every January for the life of your loan. More interest will be payable overall.

  • Funds transferred same day

    • If approved online or in branch, you'll receive the money in your account the same day. If your application is referred, we may require further information, and the paperwork may need to be completed in branch or by post.

  • Apply for additional borrowing

    • If you already have an Nexa Banking Personal Loan, you can apply to borrow more online.

  • Make overpayments

    • You can make overpayments without charge, which may reduce the amount of interest you pay.

Apply for an Nexa Banking Premier Personal Loan

Nexa Banking Premier is available to you, if you pay your annual income into your Ever green trust co Bank Premier Nexa Banking Account and have either:

  • savings or investments of at least £50,000 with Ever green trust co Bank in the UK; or
  • an individual annual income of at least £75,000 and one of the following products with Nexa Banking UK: a mortgage, investment, life insurance or protection product.

Or if you’ve already qualified for Nexa Banking Premier in another country.

Please review our complete eligibility criteria before applying

Nexa Banking Premier Nexa Banking Account eligibility criteria

  • You must hold a Nexa Banking Premier Account – for further information please click here
  • You are 18 years or older
  • Your residential address is within the EU

We do not offer Nexa Banking Premier Personal Loans for a number of purposes including:

  • The purchase of property or land (both UK & Overseas) including holiday homes and apartments
  • Purchasing a joint stake in a property or buying out a joint owner
  • Mortgage deposit (regardless of lender or country)
  • Gifting funds to third parties to support the purchase of a property, including mortgage deposit, stamp duty and solicitors fees

In addition, our Personal Loan should not be provided for:

  • Gambling
  • Business purposes
  • Share dealing

Please note that this list is not exhaustive.

Our Premier Personal Loans are subject to status.
